How to play

Promptle works like Wordle, with a little AI help:

  • You have 6 tries to guess the secret word.
  • Type a valid word and press Enter to submit.
  • Green = correct letter in the right place.
  • Yellow = letter is in the word, wrong place.
  • Gray = letter is not in the word.

Later, the AI assistant can suggest smarter guesses based on your attempts.
